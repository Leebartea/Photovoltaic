# Formal Study Work Pack Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains the `Formal study work pack` export.

It is the first dedicated handoff artifact for the separate formal-study surface.

The same surface now also has a `Formal study data sheet` CSV when the next team needs structured rows instead of narrative prose.

## What It Does

The work pack gathers the live bounded-study basis into one TXT handoff:

- working surface
- study basis and study track
- protection, export, relay, and transfer posture
- packet stage and case status
- formal study intake checklist
- formal study screening snapshot
- application reference and named owners
- formal study scope summary
- formal study items that must leave the app
- key bounded study rows still being carried from the app
- board / source carry-through for the same handoff

## Why It Exists

The normal feeder brief and study sheet are still useful, but once a job has genuinely crossed into external study territory, teams need one explicit package that says:

- what the app already carried faithfully
- what must now be completed outside the app
- who owns the next controlled handback

That is the role of this export.

## Where To Use It

Use it when the project has entered:

- `Plant Engineering Surface`
- `Utility / Mini-Grid Engineering Surface`
- external relay / protection review
- external study pack
- authority-facing interconnection or witness scope

## Honest Boundary

This work pack is not the study itself.

It is the controlled handoff into the separate study surface.
