# Utility Case Timeline Export Guide

This guide explains the utility case timeline now carried inside the packet summary and packet exports.

## What It Adds

The packet lane can now carry a structured case timeline with rows such as:

- `Case progression posture`
- `Filed / review basis`
- `Current controlled revision`
- `Open comment cycle`
- `Next controlled handback`
- `Witness / closeout track`

## Why It Exists

Status alone is not enough on an approval-heavy job.

The export also needs to preserve:

- what stage the case is really in
- what has already been filed or reviewed
- what the current controlled revision is
- whether the case is still inside an open comment loop
- what the next handback is
- how the witness or closeout path is expected to continue

That is what this timeline adds.

## Where It Appears

This timeline now appears in:

- the on-screen packet scaffold
- the packet TXT brief
- the packet CSV data sheet

## Honest Boundary

This improves revision and review-state continuity.

It does not replace:

- formal correspondence control
- a utility portal
- a document-management system
- final interconnection engineering responsibility
