# Formal Study Screening Snapshot Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains the `Formal study screening snapshot` block.

It exists to pin the most important live screening rows into the separate formal-study surface.

## What It Carries

The snapshot can lift keyed rows such as:

- `Fault reference screening`
- `AC breaker carry margin`
- `AC breaker surge screening`
- `Generator source screening`
- `Generator limiting-phase screening`
- `PV DC window screening`
- `Battery DC protection screening`
- `AC feeder cable-path screening`

## Why It Exists

The full study sheet already carries many bounded screening rows.

The screening snapshot makes the kickoff team see the highest-signal rows first, without mining the larger study sheet or work pack.

## Where It Shows Up

The same snapshot now appears in:

- the live `Plant / Mini-Grid Scope` result
- the `Formal study work pack`
- the `Formal study data sheet`

## Honest Boundary

This is still a bounded screening carry-through.

It does not replace a feeder-fault study, selectivity study, or final interconnection calculation.
