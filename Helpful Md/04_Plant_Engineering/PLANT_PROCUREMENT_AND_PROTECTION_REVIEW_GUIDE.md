# Plant Procurement And Protection Review Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains the `Board procurement / breaker / cable review` block inside the plant-scoping result.

It exists to keep the plant promise tied to the real:

- breaker path
- cable path
- board schedule
- one-line preparation

without claiming the app already completed a full protection or selectivity study.

## What It Uses

This block reuses the live sizing results already calculated elsewhere:

- plant scope
- feeder schedule
- cable sizing
- protection sizing
- phase configuration

That matters because it is not a second hidden procurement model.

## What It Surfaces

Typical review prompts include:

- PV input isolation
- battery DC protection
- AC feeder breaker carry-through
- PV field cabling
- battery DC cabling
- AC feeder cabling
- cable hard checks
- protection review notes
- three-phase board carry-through
- board schedule discipline

## How To Use It

Use it when the project is moving from:

- commercial scoping

into:

- procurement
- board naming
- breaker tagging
- cable takeoff
- one-line preparation

## Honest Boundary

This block is useful for:

- preventing mismatch between quote and board schedule
- carrying breaker sizes into procurement review
- carrying cable sizing into board and feeder prep
- preserving protected vs assisted vs outside-promise lanes

It is not a substitute for:

- final protection grading
- selectivity studies
- stamped one-line diagrams
- final utility interconnection review
