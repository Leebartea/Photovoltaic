# Formal Study Data Sheet Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains the `Formal study data sheet` CSV export.

It is the structured companion to the `Formal study work pack`.

## What It Carries

The CSV can include:

- project identity
- working surface
- study basis and study track
- progression gate
- formal study scope summary
- formal study scope items
- formal study intake summary and checklist rows
- formal study screening summary and pinned screening rows
- study trace rows like reference, owner, POC / feeder / node ref, fault level, and next action
- bounded carried study rows from the app
- board / source rows and carry tags

## Best Use

Use the TXT work pack when a person needs a readable external-study handoff.

Use the CSV when the next team needs:

- engineering trackers
- consultant spreadsheets
- formal handback registers
- external study coordination sheets

## Honest Boundary

This export does not mean the app has performed the final feeder-fault, selectivity, or interconnection study.

It means the app now hands those next steps off in a structured way.
