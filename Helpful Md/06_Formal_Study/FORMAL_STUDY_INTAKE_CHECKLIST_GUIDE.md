# Formal Study Intake Checklist Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains the `Formal study intake checklist` block.

It exists to make kickoff quality visible before the external study leaves the app.

## What It Checks

The checklist can now gate:

- `Reference / trace discipline`
- `Study ownership / node basis`
- `Protection / export control basis`
- `Deliverable pack readiness`
- `Next handback control`

## Why It Matters

The formal-study surface now has:

- scope definition
- a readable work pack
- a structured data sheet

The intake checklist closes the remaining gap:

- is the external-study kickoff actually disciplined yet?

## Honest Boundary

This checklist does not replace the study.

It improves whether the study starts on a controlled basis.
