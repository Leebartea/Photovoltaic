# Utility Stage Packet Template Guide

This guide explains the stage-template packet packs now carried by the utility packet lane.

## What It Adds

The packet lane no longer exports only abstract stage notes.

It now also exports the packet bundle emphasis for the active stage.

Examples:

- `Draft packet cover set`
- `Draft technical basis set`
- `Submission cover pack`
- `Submission technical dossier`
- `Review-response pack`
- `Review closure matrix`
- `Conditional clearance closeout pack`
- `Witness / acceptance pack`

## Why It Matters

An approval-heavy job usually fails in handoff when everyone knows the stage but nobody knows what the actual packet should contain at that stage.

This template layer fixes that by saying:

- what bundle should exist now
- what kind of content belongs in it
- what control emphasis matters at this stage

## Where It Appears

This template layer now appears in:

- the on-screen packet scaffold
- the packet TXT brief
- the packet CSV data sheet

## Honest Boundary

This is still a bounded packet template layer.

It does not replace:

- stamped authority forms
- EPC document control
- final interconnection engineering
- a utility submission portal
