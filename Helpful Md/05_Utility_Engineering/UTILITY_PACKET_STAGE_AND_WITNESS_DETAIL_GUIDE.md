# Utility Packet Stage And Witness Detail Guide

The utility lane now carries more than packet lane, study basis, and commissioning path.

It also carries:

- `Packet Stage`
- `Witness Parties`
- `Witness Evidence`

That gives the heavier lane a more realistic approval and acceptance story without turning the normal commercial workflow into a full utility package tool.

## Packet Stage

Use this to show how mature the packet really is.

Typical states:

- planning / draft basis
- ready for submission
- submitted / under review
- conditional / pending closeout
- external case managed

Use it to answer:

- is this still internal preparation?
- has it already left proposal work?
- is an external authority or EPC now controlling the case?

## Witness Parties

Use this to show who must be present in the commissioning or acceptance lane.

Typical paths:

- installer internal only
- client + installer witness
- authority / utility witness
- multi-party witness

Use it to answer:

- is this just an installer handoff?
- does the client need a formal witness?
- does a regulator, utility, or third party now matter?

## Witness Evidence

Use this to show what proof pack the job now needs.

Typical evidence levels:

- startup + label proof
- staged transfer / restart proof
- non-export / protection proof
- authority acceptance pack

Use it to answer:

- is normal startup proof enough?
- does the handoff now depend on staged restoration evidence?
- does the job need export / anti-backfeed proof?
- does the authority lane need a formal acceptance pack?

## Honest Boundary

These fields improve structure and handoff quality.

They do not turn the app into:

- a formal interconnection application system
- a completed witness-test procedure
- a full utility acceptance management platform
